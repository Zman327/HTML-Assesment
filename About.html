About Us


Flaming Pizza

Flaming Pizza, a beloved culinary gem, has been a cherished part of the vibrant Christchurch community for over two decades, having first graced the area with its delightful presence in the year 2000. Over the years, this cozy establishment has etched itself into the hearts of locals and visitors alike, becoming a go-to destination for those seeking an unforgettable dining experience.

What sets Flaming Pizza apart is our unwavering commitment to excellence in every aspect of the establishment. From the moment you step foot through their inviting doors, you are met with an ambiance that exudes warmth and a sense of belonging. The dimly lit dining area, adorned with tasteful decor and soft music, creates the perfect setting for a memorable evening filled with laughter and delicious food.
